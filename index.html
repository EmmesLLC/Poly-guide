Meeting Room Guide

Poly / Polycom Video Conferencing Setup

Connect your laptop, set your devices, and share your screen — in 6 simple steps.

1

Connect the VC Device

  1. Plug the USB cable from the Poly/Polycom VC device into your laptop.
  2. Ensure the device is powered ON.
  3. Connect the room display (TV/monitor) to the VC unit — usually HDMI, already set in meeting rooms.
2

Set the Device on Your Laptop

  1. Open your meeting app (Teams / Zoom / WebEx).
  2. Go to Settings → Devices.
  3. Select:
    • Camera: Poly Studio / Poly Camera
    • Microphone: Poly Studio / Poly Audio
    • Speaker: Poly Studio
3

Start or Join a Meeting

  1. Open Teams / Zoom / Webex.
  2. Join your meeting normally.
4

Share Your Screen

In Microsoft Teams

  1. Click Share.
  2. Choose Entire screen (recommended), or Window / PowerPoint.
  3. Click Share.

In Zoom

  1. Click Share Screen.
  2. Select your screen / window.
  3. Click Share.
5

Display on Room Screen

  1. The shared content should automatically appear on the TV connected to the Poly device.
If it doesn't show: Check the TV input (HDMI source), and make sure your laptop is not incorrectly in “extend” display mode.
6

HDMI Content ShareOptional

Some rooms allow direct sharing without a meeting:

  1. Connect the HDMI cable from the table to your laptop.
  2. Press the Content / Share button on the Poly remote (if available).
  3. Your screen will display instantly.
